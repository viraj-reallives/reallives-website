What is RealLives?

RealLives is an immersive life simulation game that puts you in the shoes of people from around the world. Born into different countries, families, and circumstances, you'll make decisions that shape your virtual life while learning about the real-world factors that influence human experiences.

Authentic Life Experiences

Live authentic lives based on real demographic and economic data from countries worldwide.

Real-World Decision Making

Face realistic choices about education, career, health, and relationships that mirror real-world constraints.

Global Perspective

Experience life in over 195 countries with accurate cultural, economic, and social contexts.

Educational Benefits

Build Empathy

Experience life through different perspectives and develop deep understanding of diverse human experiences.

Global Awareness

Understand global inequalities, cultural differences, and the interconnectedness of our world.

Critical Thinking

Develop analytical skills through complex decision-making in realistic scenarios.

Social Responsibility

Understand the impact of individual choices on communities and society as a whole.

RealLives Foundation

Empowering global citizens through immersive life simulation and educational experiences that build empathy, understanding, and changemaker skills.
