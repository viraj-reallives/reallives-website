RealLives is the Drosophila of
Human Experience Simulations

In just 45–60 minutes, RealLives lets users live a full, data-driven life in another part of the world, making choices, facing challenges, and reflecting on privilege, inequality, and chance. Just as Drosophila is the model organism for genetics, RealLives is the model simulation for global empathy and social understanding, revealing the “DNA” of human society through lived experience.
